Sociologists on Mastodon

Follow all your favourite sociologists

You want to find your sociological network in Mastodon? This page lists sociological accounts and offers an easy method to follow multiple of those accounts in a few clicks. For this you are provided with a .csv-file, based on your choices. Mastodon allows to follow muliple accounts by uploading this file. Here you can find an short instruction, on how to do this.

Here you have two options, either you can download the complete list, or you can select specific accouts you want to follow. A csv-file will be downloaded once you press the button.
