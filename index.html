Sociologists on Mastodon

Follow all your favourite sociologists

You want to find your sociological network in Mastodon? This page lists sociological accounts and offers an easy method to follow multiple of those accounts in a few clicks. For this you are provided with a .csv-file, which you can upload in Mastodon to follow all accounts you chose. Here you can find an short instruction, on how to do this.
